Korean Chicken Recipe

Home

Ingredients

  • chicken Thighs 500g
  • gochujang paste
  • honey
  • soy sauce
  • garlic powder
  • smoked paprika
  • onion powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • flour
  • sesame seeds

Steps:

  1. Dice chicken thighs into cubes
  2. mix garlic powder, smoked paprika, onion power, salt and pepper, and flour into a large bowl
  3. add diced chicken to the bowl and mix until thoroughly coated
  4. fry chicken in a deep fat frier or pan until golden brown
  5. mix a tbsp of gochujang paste and honey, and 2 tbsp soy sauce in a frying pan
  6. add the chicken to the mixture and cook on low heat until the chicken is thoroughly coated
  7. add sesame seeds and serve
